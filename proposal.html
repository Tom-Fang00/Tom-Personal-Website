Project Proposal

  • What is the website about?

    • The website is about an imaginary restaurant named "Shao Lin". The restaurant will feature a "Zen" theme and will likely be vegetarian.

  • What is the objective of the website?

    • The website is just for general information of the restaurant, to educate potential customers about the culture and food of "Shao Lin".

  • Who is your target audience?

    • Target audience will be all those that want to experience some high end vegetarian cuisine.

  • What content do you plan to include in each of your website?

    • There will be a main page that serves as a directory. There will be a meet the team and the menu option from the main page. From the menu page, there will be a list of menus for different days and each direct to a new page of different menu. There will also be a not functional reservation page.
